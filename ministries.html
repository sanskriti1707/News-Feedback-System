Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare

It is a branch of the Government of India and the apex body for formulation and administration of the rules and regulations and laws related to agriculture in India.

Ministry of Water Resources

This Department is mainly responsible for laying down policy guidelines for the development, conservation and management of water as a national resource.

Ministry of Finance

It is a ministry within the Government of India concerned with the economy of India, serving as the Treasury of India.

Ministry of Education

It is a ministry of the Government of India, responsible for the implementation of the National Policy on Education.

Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate change

This ministry is responsible for planning, promoting, coordinating, and overseeing the implementation of environmental and forestry programmes.

Ministry of Road Transport and Highways

This is the apex body for formulation and administration of the rules and laws relating to road transport, transport research to increase the mobility and efficiency of the road transport system.
